Why use QET?

Explore the core functionalities of your software.

Advanced Conductor and Connection Management


The program incorporates improved conductor management through a placement algorithm that uses pathfinding functions.

Powerful Element Editor


The Element Editor allows you to create and modify components with precision including options to ease the duplication of parts.

Smart Automatic Numbering


QET offers versatile automatic numbering functions for conductors and components to ensure consistency throughout your projects.

Extensive Library


With thousands of library components readily available, start to work right away and translate your ideas into technical documents quickly.
